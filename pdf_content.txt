Proposed Solution 
PROPOSED SOLUTION OVERVIEW
PolicyLens is an AI-powered platform that
improves government scheme management.
It analyzes data to detect fraud, leakage &
irregularities.
Provides a centralized dashboard for real-time
monitoring.
Uses automation to reduce manual work &
delays.
Tracks fund flow & beneficiary data in real-
time.
Provides data-driven insights for better
decisions.
Works as a single unified dashboard for
schemes.
